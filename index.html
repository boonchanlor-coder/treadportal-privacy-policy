Privacy Policy for TreadPortal

Last updated: April 17, 2026

TreadPortal respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account information (email, username)
  • Profile content you create
  • Photos, videos, and media you upload
  • Messages and interactions within the platform

How We Use Information

  • To operate and improve the app
  • To enable social features and messaging
  • To ensure safety and prevent abuse

Account Deletion

You can delete your account in the app under Settings → Manage Account → Delete Account.

When you request deletion, your account is scheduled for permanent deletion after 30 days.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.
