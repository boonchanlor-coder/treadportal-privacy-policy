Privacy Policy for TreadPortal

Last updated: April 17, 2026

TreadPortal ("we," "our," or "us") is an automotive social community app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address and account information
  • Profile details (username, bio, photos)
  • Content you create (posts, comments, messages)
  • Media you upload (photos and videos)
  • Usage activity (likes, follows, interactions)

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate and improve the app
  • To enable social features and messaging
  • To maintain safety and prevent abuse
  • To provide customer support

3. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and storage
  • Google Sign-In — optional login method
  • Google Places API — location search
  • Sentry — crash reporting and diagnostics

4. Account Deletion

You can delete your account in the app under Settings → Manage Account → Delete Account.

When requested, your account is scheduled for permanent deletion after 30 days. During this period, you may log back in to cancel deletion.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.

6. Children's Privacy

TreadPortal is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Update your profile
  • Delete your account

8. Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections and secure authentication systems.

9. Contact

Email: support@treadportal.app
